Building The Future of Recruitment

RecruitIQ helps growing teams run structured hiring with ATS management, interview workflows, and hiring analytics in one modern platform.

Our Story

RecruitIQ was founded to solve the real hiring challenges faced by recruiters and HR teams every day. Traditional recruitment processes are slow, fragmented, and nearly impossible to measure effectively.

We built a platform that combines ATS workflows, structured candidate evaluation, interview scheduling, analytics, and automation into one cohesive recruitment solution — so hiring teams can focus on what matters: finding the right people.
